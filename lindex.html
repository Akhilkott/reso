ABOUT US
Don Bosco College of Arts and Science, a unit of Don Bosco Group of educational Institutions Chitradurga, Affiliated to the University of Davangere, is a place of higher learning in the field of Commerce, Management and Computer Science. The Primary aim of this college is to develop the intellectual potential of its students and to form them to become intelligent contributors in their core areas, by grooming them as top class professionals to excel in their lives as honest and responsible citizens.
